Molfar Comics is a Ukrainian publisher specializing in the release of comics, manga, and Korean webtoons in Ukrainian. It targets a wide audience — from school-age readers to adults — and actively expands its catalog of Asian content. The publisher focuses on popular and recognizable series that have already gained the love of readers worldwide.

Molfar Comics pays attention to modern publication design and accessibility for new readers who are just discovering the world of manga and webtoons. Through the active development of its catalog, the publisher is gradually becoming an important part of the Ukrainian market for Asian graphic literature.

## Features

- Publishing manga and Korean webtoons
- Focus on popular series
- Modern approach to design
- Accessibility for new readers
- Active catalog development
