Mal'opus is a Ukrainian publishing house specializing in comics, graphic novels, and manga released in the Ukrainian language. The publisher actively develops translated graphic literature and introduces readers to well-known international series, author-driven works, and cult publications. It targets both experienced comic fans and newcomers who are just discovering the medium.

Mal'opus pays special attention to translation quality, printing standards, and preserving the atmosphere of the original editions. Thanks to a wide range of genres — from adventure stories to science fiction and superhero narratives — the publisher continues to build a diverse catalog and makes an important contribution to the development of comic reading culture in Ukraine.

## Features
- Publishing comics, graphic novels, and manga
- Localization of popular international series
- High-quality translation and printing
- Wide genre diversity
- Promotion of comic culture in Ukraine
