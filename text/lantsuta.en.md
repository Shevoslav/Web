Lantsuta Publisher is an independent Ukrainian publishing house that is gradually but confidently entering the manga and modern graphic storytelling market. Unlike major players, it focuses on niche and lesser-known projects that are hard to find at other publishers. The team actively searches for unique content that can appeal both to experienced manga readers and those who are just beginning to explore this format.

Lantsuta Publisher aims to expand the selection available to Ukrainian readers and prove that the manga market in Ukraine is not limited to mainstream series alone. The publisher plays an important role in bringing diversity to the Ukrainian market and opens up new names and stories for audiences that were previously unavailable in their native language.

## Features

- Independent publisher
- Niche and lesser-known titles
- Promising new releases
- Focus on market development
- Search for unique content
