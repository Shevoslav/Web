Nasha Idea is a Ukrainian publisher specializing in officially licensed manga and is one of the leaders on the Japanese comics market in Ukraine. The publisher works directly with Japanese rights holders, ensuring high-quality and legal editions in the Ukrainian language.

Nasha Idea pays special attention to the quality of translation and adaptation, preserving the spirit of the original material. Regular new releases, popular worldwide titles, and active communication with fans have made the publisher a true cornerstone of the Ukrainian manga community. Thanks to their work, more and more readers are able to enjoy their favorite series in their native language.

## Features

- Officially licensed manga
- Quality translation and adaptation
- Regular new releases
- Popular worldwide titles
- Active communication with fans
