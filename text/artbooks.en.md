Artbooks is a Ukrainian publishing house specializing in illustrated books, artbooks, manga, and publications related to popular culture. It is known for adapting international bestsellers and releasing books connected to films, animation, video games, and Japanese pop culture for Ukrainian readers.

The publisher combines high artistic design standards with careful attention to visual presentation. Artbook focuses on fans of art, anime, manga, and modern pop culture, steadily expanding its catalog and strengthening its position in the Ukrainian book market.

## Features
- Publishing artbooks and manga
- Books based on films, games, and animation
- High-quality illustrated editions
- Focus on pop culture audiences
- Continuous expansion of visual publications
